Get To Know Us

TOBELLO GLOBAL RESOURCES LTD is a corporation duly and certifiably incorporated under the Federal Republic of Nigeria's Companies and Allied Matters
Act,1990, by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry (Co-operative Office-ABUJA STATE).
Its Founder is Mr. Tobi Ayoola Bello. The director of TOBELLO INTEGRATED SERVICE LTD. has brought together a highly respected group of individuals who are
well-versed in Foreign trade processes.
Tobello Global Resources Ltd is one of Nigeria's leading export and import service providers. We are involved in the international trade of Agro Agricultural
Products and Foodstuffs (such as Fresh Honey and beeswax, Natural Sesame Seed )(White/Brown 98 percent Cleaned), Ginger-Cleaned DrySplit/Slice, Garlic,
Gum Arabic-1,2 & 3, Cassava Chips/Starch/Flour, Shea Butter/Nut, Groundnut (Peanut)/ However, we are currently focused on the collection and export of cattle
horns and hooves (Bovine) for organic fertilizer. It is a rapidly expanding industry in Europe and Asia.... We produce large amounts of crushed horns and hooves for
such an organic fertilizer industry.
